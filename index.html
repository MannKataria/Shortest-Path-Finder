In this topic, We are going to discuss the shortest path finding problems by graph theory in discrete mathematics. We will present a greedy algorithm discovered by the Dutch mathematician Edsger Dijkstra in 1959. It will help us for finding the shortest way between two points or two places. It also helps a lot in our regular life for finding the shortest path.
